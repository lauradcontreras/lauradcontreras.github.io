I am a Ph.D. candidate at the Aix-Marseille School of Economics (Aix-Marseille University), supervised by Lorenzo Rotunno and Federico Trionfetti. My research focuses on spatial economics, labor, and development, with a particular emphasis on informality, firms, and place-based policies in developing countries. I am also exploring the effects of trade and innovation in these contexts.
