I am a Ph.D. candidate at the Aix-Marseille School of Economics (Aix-Marseille University) under the supervision of Lorenzo Rotunno and Federico Trionfetti.
My research focuses on spatial economics, labor, and development, with a particular emphasis on informality, firms, and place-based policies in developing countries. Another part of my recent work addresses the consequences of trade and innovation.
