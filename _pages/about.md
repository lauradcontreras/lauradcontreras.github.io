Welcome to my website! 

I'm Laura Contreras-Portela, Ph.D. Candidate in Economics at Aix Marseille School of Economics (AMSE). 

I'm currently working on Place-Based Policies (PBPs). I am exploring their effects on informality, intrahousehold allocation, and housing quality in a spatial economy. 

My primary research and policy interests are labor economics, spatial economics, and macro-development.
