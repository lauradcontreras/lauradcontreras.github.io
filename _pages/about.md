My research projects focus on the economics of industrial pollution, exploiting both theory and an extensive range of administrative data on French plants. His job market paper analyses the welfare effects of the spatial distribution of polluting activities and the role of local air quality policies. He also works on the impacts of the EU ETS when controlling for the incompleteness of the regulation.
